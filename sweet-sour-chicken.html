Sweet and Sour Chicken Thighs

Description:

This a family-friendly dinner that is as good as takeout but made really easy in your slow cooker. Goes really well with rice

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons cornstarch, divided
  • 1/4 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 and a half pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 can (20 ounce) pineapple chunks in juice
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  1. Mix 2 tablespoons cornstarch, garlic powder, and ground ginger together in a small bowl.
  2. Toss chicken thighs in cornstarch mixture until they are evenly coated
  3. Heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken in the skillet until browned on all sides, 3 minutes per side. Transfer to a plate and set aside.
  4. Drain juice from the can of pineapple chunks into a measuring cup. You shoul have 3/4 cup to 1 cup of juice.
  5. Mix 1/2 cup pineapple juice, chicken stock, rice vinegar, brown sugar, ketchup, and soy sauce together in a slow cooker.
  6. Add browned chicken pieces and stir to coat chicken with sauce. Cook on low for 3 and a half hours.
  7. Whisk remaining 2 tablespoons cornstarch with remaining 1/4 cup pineapple juice. Stir cornstarch mixture into the slow cooker. INcrease the slow cooker heat to High and add pineapple chunks and chopped bell pepper.
  8. Cook on High until the sauce is bubbly and thickened and bell pepper is crisp-tender, about 30 minutes. Garnish with chopped green onion.
