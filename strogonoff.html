Strogonoff

Description:

A really tasty beef strogonoff that will make you wish you could eat more.

This dish is easy and fun to cook. It should tak you 10 minutes to prepare and another 10 to cook it.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Half teaspoon garlic powder
  • Half teaspoon salt
  • Half teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 4 ounces egg noodles, cooked and drained

Steps:

  1. Cook the noodles, drain and reserve.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the beef in oil over medium high heat
  3. When the meat is browned, drain excess fat from skillet.
  4. Add garlic powder, salt and pepper and stir in.
  5. Add bouillon, onion and mushrooms tom skillet and saute until the onions are translucent
  6. Remove from heat and add sour cream
  7. Stir everything together with the noodles.
